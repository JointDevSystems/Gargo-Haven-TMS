About the Company

Jastar Logistics Limited

Jastar Logistics Limited is a Mombasa-based empty container logistics company operating for nearly a decade. Headquartered at Pwani Plaza, Kilindini — in the heart of Kenya's premier port city — we are a specialist in the movement of empty containers across depots, port, CFS facilities, and client premises.

What We Do

Our Core Services

We specialise exclusively in empty container logistics — the critical backbone of Kenya's import-export supply chain. Every route, every depot, every container type.

🏭

Depot to Depot

Seamless transfer of empty containers between depots — Hakika, Dodwel, Fortune, and all Mombasa stacking yards. Optimising repositioning and minimising idle time.

High Volume
⚓

Depot to Port

Timely delivery of empty containers from storage depots to the Kenya Ports Authority — coordinated with port schedules and vessel cut-off windows.

Port Operations
🔄

Port to Depot

Collection and repatriation of empty containers from the Port of Mombasa back to designated depots — keeping container inventory balanced across the chain.

Collection
🏗️

Port to CFS

Movement of empty containers from KPA to Container Freight Stations. Fast, compliant, and coordinated with CFS receiving teams for seamless handover.

CFS Movements
🏢

Client to Depot

Direct pickup of empty containers from client premises, stuffing yards, and warehouses — delivered safely to designated stacking depots.

Client Service
🚢

Export Movements

Full positioning support for export-bound containers — from stuffing location to port gate, ensuring on-time delivery ahead of vessel cut-off deadlines.

Exports
